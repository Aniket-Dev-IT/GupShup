Cookie Policy

Learn how we use cookies to improve your experience on GupShup

Last updated: December 2024
What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our platform.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for various purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze website usage and performance
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Targeting Cookies: Provide personalized content and advertisements

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Session Cookies Keep you logged in during your visit Until browser is closed
Authentication Cookies Remember your login status 30 days
Preference Cookies Store your settings and preferences 1 year
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how you use our site 2 years
Security Cookies Protect against fraud and security threats Session/30 days

Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party cookies from trusted partners:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics and performance monitoring
  • Social Media Platforms: Social sharing and login functionality
  • Content Delivery Networks: Faster loading of website resources

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their use of cookies.

Managing Your Cookies

You have control over cookies on your device:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block some or all cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites

Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences through our Cookie Consent banner or by updating your account settings.

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your user experience.

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, we'll ask for your consent to use cookies. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
