Community Guidelines

Building a safe, respectful, and inclusive community for everyone.

Our Community Values

GupShup is built on the foundation of respect, authenticity, and meaningful connections. These guidelines help us maintain a positive environment where everyone can share, learn, and connect safely.

Be Respectful

Treat all community members with dignity and respect. Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory behavior based on race, gender, religion, or any other characteristic is not tolerated.

Stay Safe

Protect your personal information and respect others' privacy. Don't share personal details like addresses, phone numbers, or financial information publicly.

Be Authentic

Use your real identity and share genuine content. Fake accounts, impersonation, or misleading information undermines trust in our community.

Engage Positively

Contribute meaningful content and engage in constructive conversations. Spam, excessive self-promotion, or irrelevant content disrupts the community experience.

Prohibited Content

Violence or threats
Hate speech or discrimination
Harassment or bullying
Adult or explicit content
Illegal activities
Spam or excessive self-promotion
False or misleading information
Copyright infringement
Impersonation or fake accounts
Malicious links or malware

Reporting and Enforcement

How to Report

If you encounter content that violates these guidelines, please report it immediately using the report button or contact our support team.

Enforcement Actions

Violations may result in:

  • Content removal
  • Warning messages
  • Temporary restrictions
  • Account suspension
Appeals Process

If you believe an action was taken in error, you can appeal by contacting our support team within 30 days.

These guidelines are subject to change. We'll notify the community of any major updates.
