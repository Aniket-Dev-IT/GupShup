Data Protection

Your data security and privacy are our top priorities

Last updated: December 2024
Our Commitment to Data Protection

At GupShup, we are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy rights. We implement comprehensive security measures and follow industry best practices to ensure your information remains safe and secure.

Encryption

All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols. Your personal information is stored using advanced encryption methods.

Secure Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers with multiple layers of protection, including firewalls, intrusion detection, and regular security audits.

Access Control

Strict access controls ensure that only authorized personnel can access your data, and all access is logged and monitored.

Regular Monitoring

We continuously monitor our systems for security threats and vulnerabilities, with 24/7 security monitoring and incident response.

Data Collection and Usage

We collect and process your personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number for account creation
  • Profile Data: Information you choose to share on your profile
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform for improvement purposes
  • Communication Data: Messages and content you share on the platform
  • Device Information: Technical information about your device and browser

Your Data Rights

Under data protection laws, you have several rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

Correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.

Right to Restrict Processing

Limit how we process your personal data in certain situations.

Right to Data Portability

Receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to Object

Object to certain types of data processing.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in our Privacy Policy:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Communication Data: Retained according to legal requirements
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymized after 2 years
  • Security Logs: Retained for 1 year for security purposes

International Data Transfers

If we transfer your data outside of your country, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by regulatory authorities
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant data protection authorities
  • Other legally recognized transfer mechanisms

Security Incidents

In the unlikely event of a data breach:

  • We will assess the risk to your rights and freedoms
  • We will notify relevant authorities within 72 hours if required
  • We will inform affected users if there is a high risk to their rights
  • We will take immediate steps to contain and remedy the breach
